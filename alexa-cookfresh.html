STORYBOARD

SCENE 1/8

The system will accurately identify what products are available in the fridge and proactively search for recipes that contain those products. It will provide users with several options and rank them using different criteria, including user's preference, available ingredients in fridge, etc.

SCENE 2/8

Users will be able to quickly order the missing ingredients with Alexa through Amazonfresh. The system will place the order and have them delivered within hours (dependent on the efficiency of the external delivery service).

SCENE 3/8

Once the ingredients has been delivered, users can continue interacting with Alexa by providing deliver confirmation. The system will then asked users if they need cooking guidance.

SCENE 4/8

Users will have the option to follow any recipe step by step provided by Alexa. They will not have to memorize every step at once. Instead, they get instructions on what to do at the moment via voice output.

SCENE 5/8

Users will complete the whole process without having to take out their smartphone, tablet or computer and interact with a visual interface by hand.

PROTOTYPE

We created a prototype using Sayspring, an online tool for prototyping voice interactions. We have two predetermined responses for what to cook. Depending on the ingredient available, Alexa might directly suggest recipes, or notice users in advance that some ingredients are going to expire, and thus need to be cooked soon.


We tried to take care of all the edge cases in the prototype that is not mentioned in the storyboard, so that the interaction feels as natural as possible. Feel free to play around with it here. (You will need to create a Sayspring account to access the prototype)

Food preservation

What to cook

College students cannot afford spending much time on receipts search and cooking. Many of them also do not know how to cook, or have no idea what to cook with the available food ingredients.

Cooking reference

With limited time and attention to spare, college students tend to forget what they have in the fridge and therefore have to frequently throw away food that has gone bad.

A novice cook need to refer to the instructions many time at the moment while they are cooking, which increase their cognitive load as they are already preoccupied with previous cooking tasks.

?

Cook Fresh is an Alexa skill that provides suggested recipes to users based on the food they have in the fridge, and orders missing ingredients from Amazon fresh.
