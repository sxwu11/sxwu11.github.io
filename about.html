Shengxi is a UX designer with architectural design background. He has a blend of technical and visual design skills and an eye for detail. He thinks visually, using techniques like storyboards and sketching, to communicate design concepts. He thinks logically, using knowledge of the UX process, in collaboration with other team members, to iterate multiple solutions and deliver compelling designs.


While he was practicing architecture, he developed interests in designing physical experience and digital experience. UX Designer is Digital Architect. He always dreams about crafting digital space that people live, work and entertain in the future.
