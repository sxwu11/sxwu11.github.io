PROTOTYPE PREVIEW

The Resurrected is a VR experience designed for the Oculus Rift. It was inspired by Michael Jackson's Thriller MTV. Interactors will resurrect from a coffin with other zombies, realize their identity, follow the invitation and dancing tutorial provided by Michael Jackson, and join a group zombie dance in the end.


This is an on-going project. We just completed our 3rd round of user testing with our minimum viable product. We will summarize user feedbacks, come up with design implications and refine our narrative and interaction design. We are hoping to complete this project by early December this year.

MY ROLE

As the only member who had previous experience with virtual reality, I came up with the innovative narrative with the capability and limitation of VR in mind while discussing with Tony, and then pitched it to the class.


During the initial ideation phase, I brainstormed a lot of creative spacial/architectural ideas and improve VR immersion and user experience. I was responsible for all the assets, including texture, model and animations throughout the Unity development phase. With my HCI background, I evaluated the experience through 3 rounds of testing, and kept iterating on the narrative with my teammates.
